A Perfect Repair

How to perform a perfect repair:

  1. Acquiring necessary equipment and supplies.
  2. Removing damaged, flaking, or deteriorated parts of the vessel.
  3. Removing protective coating around the repair area.
  4. Rebuilding damaged area with appropriate fiberglass, resin, and compounds.
  5. Recreating the original shape of the vessel with fairing.
  6. Applying new protective coating over the repair area.
